Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Scorepad is built as a tool to help you track scores for your favorite games. Your privacy is paramount to us, and we have designed this app with that in mind.

1. Data Collection

Scorepad does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Since the application functions 100% offline, all information you enter into the application (such as game names and scores) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData technology.

2. Data Usage

Since we do not collect any data, your information never leaves your device and is not used for any marketing, analytics, or third-party purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Scorepad does not use any third-party SDKs or services that collect user data. We use Apple's standard frameworks (UIKit, SwiftUI, SwiftData) to provide the app experience.

4. Children's Privacy

Because Scorepad does not collect any personal information, it is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will reflect the revision.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at avadlapatla.apps+scorepad@gmail.com.
