Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new game?

Tap the prominent "+" button on the home screen. You can then enter the game name and player names to get started.

Can I track more than 6 players?

Currently, Scorepad supports up to 6 players per game session to ensure the interface remains clear and usable on all device sizes.

How do I edit a player's name?

Player names are set when creating a new game. If you made a mistake, you can currently start a new game session with the correct names.

Does Scorepad require an internet connection?

No! Scorepad is designed to be 100% offline. You can track scores anywhere—on a plane, in the mountains, or in your own backyard—without needing Wi-Fi or cellular data.

Is my data backed up?

Scorepad stores data locally on your device. If you delete the app, your game history will be removed. We are considering iCloud sync for a future update.
